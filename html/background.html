BACKGROUND

From Slovakia, his birth place, Palo acquired his broad knowledge about floristry, botanics and management. Shortly after finishing his studies he moved to Ireland where he spent 10 years of his live, improving his technique and gathering an interesting professional network.

An endless fight to achieve his dream of becoming one of the most renowned florists in the industry, pushed him to move to London where he found himself like at home. In just three years living in the British capital, Palo has positioned himself amongst the best, he has developed his personal brand and his name is known in the high spheres of the industry. Owner of his own start-up and freelance for the big names of the floristry in the UK. In this stage Palo also worked as a florist for popular movies like “The Counselor” or “Into the Woods”

Always eager to find new knowledge to apply to his floral creations, Palo carries a valuable background in interior design and arts that gave him a special interest in contemporary trends. Passion for architecture and his interior design experience, leads him to achieve incomparable floral creations designed to cleverly enhance the shapes, spaces and geometry of some of the most amazing venues all over Europe. Crafting techniques are also at the forefront of his design work and his extreme ability, fast pace of work and perfectionism led him to top of his field where he proudly stands nowadays.

His future projects will guide him to New York, city in which he always wanted to succeed and also teaching. Palo is planning to start as a one-to-one floristry teacher for exclusive clients, and is planning to release his first book very soon.
