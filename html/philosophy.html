PHILOSOPHY

Palo’s philosophy is simple yet powerful: elegance, rich simplicity and innovation.

From his point of view, flowers should be treated as ephemeral pieces of art that should get blended within the space in which they are displayed, creating a feeling of unavoidable naturalness. Thanks to this guidelines Palo achieves with ease a spontaneous balance between visual stimulation, olfactory experience and the adequate style for the specific event or occasion.

Flowers are Palo’s life, his passion, his muses.
